About Me

Turning data into
real-world impact

"I build AI systems that ship. Not just slide decks."

Over the past 5 years I have built production ML pipelines, LLM-powered agents, and data systems at BMW, Walmart, and VISABLE, alongside 150+ client projects on Fiverr and Upwork. The work ranges from forecasting models that lifted sales by 17% to AI agents that replaced entire manual workflows, and I genuinely enjoy both ends of that spectrum.

Most recently at BMW Financial Services, I built AI agents on MCP servers using LangGraph that retrieve, analyze, and act on live operational data across Jira and Confluence. My experience covers the full stack of applied AI, from time series forecasting and NLP to RAG pipelines, computer vision, and end-to-end MLOps on GCP, AWS, and Kubernetes. What I care about most is not the technology itself but what it makes possible for the people using it.
